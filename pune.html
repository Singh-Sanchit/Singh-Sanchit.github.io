SMART CITY - PUNE

Traffic Curbing

With the help of IOT sensors and cameras we are trying to continuously gather data regarding the vehicles movement and these details are used for producing optimal route for vehicles to avoid traffic congestion.

Smart Street Lights

This project refers to the public street light that adjusts according to the motion of the pedestrians and cars. This also dims when no activity is detected and brightens when motion is detected.

Public Transit Management

In this project we are focusing on getting the real time location of the public transports which will benefit the commuters. This is done by IOT sensors present in the vehicles which will transmit the data into smart phones used by people.
